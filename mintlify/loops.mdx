## The Quality Loop Stack

{/* Keep the named-loop capitalization (Inner Loop, Middle Loop, Meta Loop, Outer Loop). */}

- **Discover** (∞)
- **Learn** (≈) — improve by refining the quality model
  and raising the bar, compounding learning and maximizing leverage.
- **Generate** (≈ daily) — evaluate the quality of your project by generating quality ratings, assessment-based findings, and recommendations for your inner loops to act on.
- **Iterate** (≈ hourly) — act on handoff-ready recommendations as you
  work, with the model as shared context.

> The three-loop framing and the term _middle loop_ draw on Annie Vella,
> _[The Middle Loop](https://annievella.com/posts/the-middle-loop/)_. The
> loop-engineering framing also draws on Latent Space,
> _[Loopcraft: The Art of Stacking Loops](https://www.latent.space/p/ainews-loopcraft-the-art-of-stacking)_.

## Run your first loop

{/* TODO: Walk through running the loop on a real project end to end, harness-agnostic. Expand each step. */}

You run the loop through the `/quality` skill, harness-agnostic. One pass
through all four steps is your first loop.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Evaluate">
    Run `/quality evaluate` to produce an evaluation report — quality ratings,
    assessment findings, and recommendations across your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review">
    Read ratings, findings, and recommendations by area or factor. Decide what
    matters now.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Act">
    Implement or delegate handoff-ready recommendations to people, agents, or
    other agent loops.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Improve">
    Run `/quality improve` to refine `QUALITY.md` so the next loop starts from a
    sharper model.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Then run it again. The model you sharpen in _Improve_ is what your next loop
reads from — that compounding is the point.
