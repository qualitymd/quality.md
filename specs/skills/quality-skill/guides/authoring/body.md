# QUALITY.md body authoring guide

Governs [`skills/quality/guides/authoring/body.md`](../../../../../skills/quality/guides/authoring/body.md).

The guide **MUST** cover the Markdown body as evaluable judgment context: root
Area identity, why quality matters, decisions the model supports, Needs, Risks,
unknowns, open questions, and inaccessible context.

The guide **MUST** teach the recommended body sections, the common section shape,
section-scoped unknowns and open questions, agent-accessible support, review
provenance, the governing sense of "good", stakeholder attribution, and the
distinction between Scope, unknowns, open questions, low-confidence assessments,
and `not assessed` results.
